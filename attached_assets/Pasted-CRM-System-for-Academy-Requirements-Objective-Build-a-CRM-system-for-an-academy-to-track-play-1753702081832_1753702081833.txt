CRM System for Academy - Requirements

Objective:
Build a CRM system for an academy to track players, their activities, payments, and subscription status. The system should also provide session reminders and allow easy printing of receipts.

Player Registration: Player Details:
Full Name

Activity (e.g., Karate, Kickboxing, Football, Swimming, Zumba, Aerobics, CrossFit, Gymnastics, Quran Memorization, Kindergarten)

Date of Birth

Documents (e.g., scanned copy of ID, medical form)

Subscription Date

Renewal Date

The registration form should allow editing and updating the player details at any time.

Subscription and Payment Tracking: Payment Information:
Subscription Fee (total amount)

Amount Paid

Remaining Balance

Payment Method:

Cash

Visa

Bank Transfer

The system should automatically track the payment status and notify when a player has an outstanding balance.

Session Reminders: Subscription Alerts:
Players should be notified before their renewal date, with a reminder in the 7th session for the month.

For example: “Reminder: You have 1 session left before your subscription ends. Please renew your membership to continue.”

Activity Categorization: Players should be categorized based on their selected activities:
Karate 🥋

Kickboxing 🥊

Football ⚽

Swimming 🏊🏼

Zumba

Aerobics 🏋🏻‍♂

CrossFit

Gymnastics 👯‍♀

Quran Memorization

Kindergarten

Ability to filter and sort players based on activity types for easy management and updates.

ID and Session Management: ID System:
Every player should be assigned a unique ID for easy tracking.

The ID should be used for all transactions and record keeping.

Session Flexibility:

The ability to mark sessions attended by the player.

Notify and flag players for their remaining or upcoming sessions based on their subscription.

Receipt Generation: The system should allow for the generation of receipts upon payment.
Include information such as:

Player's Name

Activity

Date of Payment

Amount Paid

Payment Method

Balance Remaining (if applicable)

The receipt should be printable or available in PDF format.

Additional Features: Dashboard View:
The admin should have a dashboard showing:

Total players

Subscription renewal status

Pending payments

Upcoming sessions

Reports and Analytics:

Ability to generate monthly or yearly reports on income, player attendance, and other metrics and use mysql database
